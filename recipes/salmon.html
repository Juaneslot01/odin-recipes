Salmon with honey and soy sauce

Finished Salmon dish

The recipe consists in 3 main steps: preparing the sauce, cooking the salmon and finally caramelize the salmon with the sauce.

Ingredients

  • Salmon strip
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix together 2 tablespoons of honey and soy sauce.
  2. Cook the salmon very well and when you think is almost ready put the mix in.
  3. Caramelize the salmon with the sauce and when it turns into a caramel color its ready to eat.
