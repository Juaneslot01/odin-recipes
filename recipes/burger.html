Burger

Finished Burger dish

The recipe consists in 4 main steps: cooking the meat, caramelize the onions, toast the bread and finally put everything together

Ingredients

  • Burger Bread
  • Burger meat
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Caramelize the onions in a pan with butter.
  2. Toast the bread in a pan with butter.
  3. Salt the meat and roast the meat in a pan and put a slice of cheese when the burger is almost done.
  4. Finally put everything together.
